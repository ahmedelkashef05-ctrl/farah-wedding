Growth Tips

📸
Add real photos — vendors with photos get 3x more inquiries. Upload at least 5 portfolio images.
⭐
Request reviews — after every wedding, ask your couple to leave a review on Farah. Vendors with 10+ reviews get priority placement.
📅
Update your availability — couples want to know if you're available for their date. Keep your calendar updated.
💬
Reply quickly — respond to inquiries within 24 hours. Couples choose vendors who respond fast.
🧵
Highlight cultural offerings — list Palestinian-specific services like tatreez, dabke, or traditional food. These are what Farah couples are looking for.
💰
Set competitive pricing — transparent pricing increases inquiries by 45%. Consider offering packages for different budgets.
